UCLA's Biggest Pitch Competition

Perfect Pitch is the biggest student pitch competition at UC Los Angeles, where over 500 students will submit video business pitches in one of three categories. The top eight finalists within each category are then invited to pitch at the Perfect Pitch Finale for cash prizes. The objective is to turn UCLA into an “ideas” campus, facilitate innovative thought, and provide opportunity for entrepreneurial problem solving that is accessible to all undergraduate students.
